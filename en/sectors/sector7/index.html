Transport & Infrastructure

Clients in the transportation industry need expert insider advice across the sector.

Our expertise and knowledge allow us to provide clients of this sector with high quality legal service. We provide a full range of legal support to transportation and logistics industry clients in the areas of financing, drafting and interpretation of regulatory requirements, public policy, mergers and acquisitions (including privatization), environmental requirements, labour and employment issues, equipment leasing, technical procurement/licensing, freight forwarders agreements, customs broker agreements.

Our team focuses on providing the highest quality legal expertise to private investors and public sector bodies involved in the acquisition, construction or operation of infrastructure and transport assets. Our clients include construction companies, developers, investors, regulatory bodies and governments.

Related projects:

  • Advising aggrieved creditors on the bankruptcy of the biggest Ukrainian shipping company for value of claims for more than USD 30 million
  • Advising a Turkish shipping agency on the claims against a Ukrainian debtor in the amount of more than USD 2.2 million
  • Advising an Egypt based shipping agency on structuring of its strategy and recovery of more than USD 24 million.
