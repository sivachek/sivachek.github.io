Medicine & Pharmaceutical

Vasko Law Office has a proven track record of successful advising of clients on contentious issues in the pharmaceutical and medical devices industry. Our comprehensive experience and thorough knowledge of specifics of this industry allows us to offer to the clients integrated decisions which take into account our clients’ interests and business-aims. We advise on every stage of creating, production and marketing of pharmaceutical products from clinical tests to licence obtaining. We also counsel our clients in regulatory, intellectual property, finance, M & A, product liability and litigations matters.
