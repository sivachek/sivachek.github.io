International Trade & Commercial Law

We have a long-standing reputation for providing comprehensive international trade legal services of exceptional quality. Our lawyers advise and represent both Ukrainian and foreign clients regarding major aspects of the customs regulations, including legal structuring of export-import operations, drafting foreign trade contracts, advising on Ukrainian customs regulation, customs duty planning in international transactions, representation before the State Customs Service of Ukraine at all levels, accreditation with the local Customs Office, and fare calculation of the customs duties for the clients benefit, parallel imports and other intellectual property issues, etc. We support clients by addressing a range of trade and regulatory issues through strategic advice, advocacy, and representation in different dispute resolution proceedings, including international commercial arbitration.

Related projects:

  • Advising world leading electronic producer on distribution structures and trade issues
  • Advising Russian Oil Group on USD 2 billion structuring of operations in Ukraine, retail and tolling arrangements, tax planning and other issues
  • Advising world biggest food producers, pharmaceutical producers and personal hygiene products producer on structuring and commercial operations in Ukraine
  • Advising world biggest producer of domestic settings on goods outbound, sale, distribution, and business structuring and operations in Ukraine
