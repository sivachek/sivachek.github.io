Real Estate, Construction & Land Law

Our Real Estate, Construction & Land Law Practice is one of the core practice areas of the firm. Our particular strength is unique involvement and understanding of real estate specifics and their local applications in Ukraine.

We provide solution-driven, cost-aware legal input at all stages of a project: on investment structuring and establishing a project entity (Special Purpose Vehicle); acquisition and/or the disposal of property; legal due diligence of property; real estate portfolios; establishment of joint ventures between foreign and local developers; negotiating and entering into lease agreements; facility management contracts (operation and maintenance of property) and/or property management contracts between landlords and hotel or restaurant operators

Our services include:

  • Acquisitions and disposals (asset and share deals)
  • Legal due diligence
  • Real estate mortgage, lease and management contracts
  • Real estate development joint ventures and agreements
  • Preliminary sale-and-purchase and lease agreements
  • General contractor agreements
  • Finance
  • Dispute resolution in construction activity
  • Legal support for construction company activities.

Related projects:

  • Advising an American Construction Company on USD 14.5 million selling of real estate assets
  • Advising an American Construction Company on USD 10 million acquisition of real estate land fill assets
  • Advising two major American Investment Funds on establishing and operation of USD 100 million real estate investment fund in Ukraine and structuring of 16 real estate projects
  • Advising a major American Investment Fund on USD 25 million agricultural land lease structuring and acquisition in Ukraine
  • Advising a European Investment Fund on USD 15 million acquisition of the land plot and development project.
