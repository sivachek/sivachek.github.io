Antimonopoly & Competition Law

In conditions of market-oriented economy and competitive environment the reasonable antimonopoly and healthy competition rules become much more important. Knowing that a successful business requires a competitive edge, our Antimonopoly & Competition Practice assists our clients in achieving their goals within the boundaries of the law. We offer our clients a full range of legal services on competition and antitrust law, including the provision of legal advice in antitrust and unfair competition matters, support in merger clearance process and obtaining approvals for concerted actions.

Effective Ukrainian competitive legislation not only prevents the consequences of a breach of the competition rules but it can be a forceful weapon in Ukraine in the event that a company’s ability to perform in the market is being unfairly restricted as a result of the activities of transnational corporations and businesses.

Our lawyers regularly advice both leading multinational and local clients on matters related to cross-border merger clearances and domestic regulatory competition issues.

We advise on the issues related to the competition law in a number of industries including banking, energy, insurance, pharmaceuticals, transportation and telecommunications industries. We carefully monitor legal and practical developments to ensure that our clients are kept informed of obligations of compliance as well as regulatory developments that may have impact on their business in Ukraine.

We advise on:

  • mergers, acquisitions and joint ventures
  • antimonopoly and other competition issues
  • competition litigation
  • contract review and compliance
